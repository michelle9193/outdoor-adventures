Camping

Once in a while we all need a break from the hustle and bustle of the city. Therefore we usggest you pack your bags and explore the Western Cape's beautiful outdoors for some peace and tranquility. Whether you're looking for a fun family getaway, a spontaneous day trip or even a romantic retreat, we've got you covered. Enjoy a braai, picnic or silent time at our State-owned resorts and camping sites which provide the perfect affordable family holiday at any time of the year. So before you pack your car and head off to a chalet near the sea or pitch your tent in the scenic mountains, have a look at the variety of holiday resorts we have to offer. Choose from a range of holiday spots that comes with all four walls or an open space under the stars to park your caravan, there are many relaxing options available to cater for your needs.
